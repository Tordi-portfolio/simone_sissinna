Simone Susinna: Model & Rising Actor
Simone Susinna is an Italian model and actor whose career has taken him from the world’s most prestigious fashion runways to the big screen. Known for his striking looks, Mediterranean charm, and magnetic presence, Simone first gained recognition as an international model working with top designers and fashion houses.

His breakthrough in acting came with the Netflix film 365 Days: This Day (2022), where he quickly became a fan favorite across the globe. His ability to blend intensity and elegance on screen positioned him as one of Europe’s most exciting new talents.

Beyond film, Simone continues to be a sought-after figure in the fashion industry, appearing in campaigns and magazine features while pursuing new creative projects. With a growing international fan base, he is carving out a unique space where fashion and cinema meet.

From modeling in Milan to acting for global audiences, Simone Susinna embodies ambition, style, and modern artistry—making him a star to watch for years to come.
