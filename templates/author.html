All About Simone Susinna
Simone Susinna is an Italian model and actor who has captivated audiences worldwide with his striking looks, charisma, and rising presence on screen. Known for his breakout role in “365 Days: This Day” on Netflix, Simone quickly became one of the most talked-about European stars of his generation.
As a fashion model, Simone has worked with international brands and graced the covers of leading magazines, building a reputation for elegance, confidence, and versatility. His transition into acting has shown his ability to bring intensity and depth to every role.
Off the runway and screen, Simone is passionate about fitness, travel, and connecting with fans through social media. His adventurous spirit and dedication to self-expression have made him an inspiration for many young people across the globe.
Born in Catania, Italy, Simone embraces his Mediterranean roots while continuing to pursue an international career. With his blend of natural charm, hard work, and ambition, he is carving out a path as both a style icon and a talented actor.
Whether stepping onto a film set, walking a fashion runway, or sharing moments from his life with millions of followers, Simone Susinna embodies passion, authenticity, and modern artistry.
"I believe in living with passion, chasing dreams, and inspiring others through my journey."
— Simone Susinna
