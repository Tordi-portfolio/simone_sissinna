Simone Susinna: Through My Lens
Ciao—I’m Simone Susinna. For me, photographs are more than just images; they capture emotions, journeys, and the lifestyle I live. From fashion runways to film sets, each photo is a glimpse into the chapters of my story.

Some of these images reflect my work as a model, walking for international brands and embracing creativity in fashion. Others capture moments from my acting career—roles that challenged me and connected me with fans across the world. And then there are the candid shots: travel, fitness, friends, and moments that remind me to live with passion and freedom.

Life has taught me that authenticity matters. I don’t chase perfection; I live in the moment, whether I’m in front of the camera, exploring new places, or enjoying the beauty of everyday life. Each of these photographs is part of that journey.

This gallery is a piece of me—an expression of my world. I hope as you scroll through these images, you see the energy, passion, and stories that define who I am.

— Simone
